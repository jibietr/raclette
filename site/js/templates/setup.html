Lights, camera, ...

Let's start by setting up the webcam and microphone for the interview. Read carefully.

1. Make sure you are using Chrome 32 and that your camera can record at 640x320.

2. Allow on the pop-up bar that appears at the top of the browser.

3. Check the webcam icon located at the address bar and make sure that you are selecting the webcam and microphone intend to use.

Note that if you change the webcam or microphone here you may be asked to reload the browser, in which case you will be sent to the first page. This is totally normal. Once your webcam and microphone are selected correctly, you won't need to change them again.

4. Check that you can see yourself and that the volume bar...
