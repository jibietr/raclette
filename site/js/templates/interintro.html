How does this interview work?

1. You will be asked several questions to be answered by video

2. Once you see the question, you will have 1 minutes to think about your response.

3. After the time is over, you will have 4 minutes to respond.

4. You don't have to use all the waiting and responding time allocated if you don't need to.

5. You cannot stop the interview once its starts, but you can pause for a bit between questions if you really need to.


What do I need?

  • A reliable internet connnection (at least 1Mb of upstream)

  • Chrome browser (at least version 32)

  • A webcam & microphone (built-in webcams and microphones are OK)

  • A quiet place

  • A well-lit room (make sure no light is directly in view of the webcam)
