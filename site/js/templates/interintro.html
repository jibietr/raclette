How does this interview work?

1. You will be asked several questions to which you will respond by video.

2. Once you see the question, you will have a limited amount of time to think about your response.

3. After the time is over, you will have a limited amount of time to respond.

4. You don't have to use all the waiting and responding time allocated. You can start and stop the recording as you want.

5. You cannot stop the interview once its starts, but you can pause a bit between questions.


What do I need?

  • A reliable internet connnection

  • Chrome browser (at least version 32)

  • A webcam & microphone (plug-in webcams and microphones are ok)

  • A quiet place

  • A well-lit room (make sure no light is directly in view of the webcam)


How do I proceed?

First, you will go through the process of setting up the webcam and microphone. Then, you will have some time to practice for the interview before you start.
